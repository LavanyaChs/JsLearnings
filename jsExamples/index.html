Inline elements don’t start on a new line, they appear on the same line as the content and tags beside them. Some examples of inline elements are 'span' , 'strong', and 'img' tags. You can add space to the left and right on an inline element, but you cannot add height to the top or bottom padding or margin of an inline element.
